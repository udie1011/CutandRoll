About

Introducing "Cut & Roll Landscaping," a budding business started by a group of industrious kids looking to make their mark in the neighborhood this summer. With a passion for plants and a knack for outdoor work, these young entrepreneurs are ready to transform lawns and gardens with their fresh perspective and dedication to quality. From mowing lawns to planting flowers, they bring enthusiasm and a commitment to excellence to every project they undertake. With competitive pricing and a friendly, can-do attitude, Sprout & Spruce is poised to become the go-to choice for local residents seeking professional landscaping services with a youthful twist. Support the next generation of green thumbs and watch your outdoor spaces flourish with Cut & Roll Landscaping!
